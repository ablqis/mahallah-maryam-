[NOTICE ON LIDV IN MAHALLAH MARYAM]
Assalamualaikum w.b.t,
Attention to all residents of Mahallah Maryam,

In light of the recent announcement on LIDV by Residential and Services Department (RSD), MRC Mahallah Maryam has been in discussion with the Mahallah Office regarding alternative methods for residents.

For reference, residents who LIDV or Leave Belongings (LB) will be charged RM4.50 per day per compartment.

The following are the alternatives for residents who would not LIDV or LB:

  • Vacate the room by clearing all belongings and returning the room-key.
  • Shared LIDV by storing items at a shared compartment under ONE name.

Priority to Sabah/Sarawak and B40 residents:
  • MPH will be available for you to put your belongings should you decide to do so.
  • Only Returning Students and Active Students are allowed to put their belongings in MPH.
  • Residents are required to fill in the online Letter of Undertaking (LOU) form that will be provided by MRC during drop-off belongings
  • All belongings must be retrieved on registration day or within 3 days after registration day.
  • All belongings that are not taken will be disposed.

All belongings during LIDV and LB at your own risk.

We hope that these alternatives ease your concerns regarding LIDV matters.
