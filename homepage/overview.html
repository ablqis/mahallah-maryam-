Mahallah Maryam Background

Mahallah Maryam was the sixth female mahallah which built on 5 hectares of land. It was completed on June 2002 and was first occupied on Semester 1, 2002/2003. It has 7 blocks of hostels, 6 for Undergraduate (UG) and another one for Postgraduate (PG) students. In full capacity the hostels could accommodate a number of 816 students at one time per semester.
